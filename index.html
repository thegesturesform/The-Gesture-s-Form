THE GESTURE'S FORM

"The Gesture's Form" explores the relationship between the body, gesture, and writing in the digital context, questioning how typography has become increasingly automated, standardized, and disconnected from the physical act of writing.

The project thus explores the possibility of reintroducing expressiveness, imperfection, and identity into digital writing, transforming reading into a performative act. By making the connection between body and text visible, it proposes a new form of interaction in which writing becomes, once again, a gesture.
